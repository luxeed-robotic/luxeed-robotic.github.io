Market Trends

The agricultural robots market was estimated at €11B in 2022 and a CAGR of 20.6% from 2023 to 2030.

The laser weeding market is experiencing significant growth, driven by:

  • The global transition toward sustainable and regenerative farming practices. The EU has goal to achieve 25% organic farming by 2030.
  • Increasing regulatory restrictions on herbicides due to their environmental and health impacts.
  • Traditional weeding methods are becoming impractical due to high labor costs (€26/hr as of 2025) and a 2% annual labor shortage.
